Mushroom Bechamel Sauce

From the Kitchen of C. Rowe



Ingredients

  • 4 Tablespoons of butter
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1 medium-sized chopped onion
  • 1 chopped garlic clove
  • 2 chopped celery stalks
  • 3 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 cups of slived mushrooms
  • 1 cup cream
  • Roux
    • 1⁄2 cup of melted butter
    • 1 cup of flour

Cooking Steps

  1. In a large pot, melt butter
  2. Add onions, celery, garlic, salt, and pepper
  3. Cook for 10 minutes
  4. Add chicken broth and heat
  5. Make roux
    • In a separate pan, melt 1⁄2 cup butter
    • Add small amounts of flour, whisking as you go to a total of 1 cup of flour
  6. Blend roux into the large pot
  7. Add 1 cup of cream and more salt and pepper to taste

This dish can be frozen and can be used as a substitute for cream of mushrom soup

Enjoy!
